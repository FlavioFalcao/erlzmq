zmq_sockopt()

zmq_sockopt() = {hwm, integer()} | {lwm, integer()} | {swap, integer()} | {affinity, integer()} | {identity, string()} | {subscribe, string()} | {unsubscibe, string()} | {rate, integer()} | {recovery_ivl, integer()} | {mcast_loop, boolean()} | {sndbuf, integer()} | {rcvbuf, integer()} | {rcvmore, boolean()} | {active, boolean()}

0MQ socket options. See 0MQ man pages for details. One additional options active indicates to the driver that incoming messages must be automatically delivered to the process owner's mailbox instead of explicitely requiring recv/1 call.
